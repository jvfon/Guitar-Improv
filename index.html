the c chromatic scale

C C# D D# E F F# G G# A A# B C

2 semi-tones equal 1 tone.

There is a tone between C and D, one tone between D and E, one semi-tone between E and F, one tone between F and G, one semi-tone between G and A, one tone between A and B and one semi-tone between B and C.

The pattern is: T T S T T T S
